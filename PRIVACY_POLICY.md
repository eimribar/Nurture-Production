## Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Name** (optional): Used for personalization of the app experience
- **Password**: Stored securely using industry-standard encryption (bcrypt hashing)

When you use our video analysis feature:
- **Video Recordings**: Videos of your baby's cries for analysis purposes
- **Audio Data**: Audio extracted from videos for cry pattern analysis
- **Analysis Results**: AI-generated insights about cry reasons and care recommendations
- **Chat Messages**: Conversations with our AI assistant

### Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, app crashes, and error reports

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts
- We do NOT collect payment information (app is free)
- We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels

### Service Improvement

### Communication

## How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

- **Google Gemini API**: Videos and audio are sent to Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash API for cry analysis
  - Data is transmitted securely via HTTPS
  - Google's use of data is governed by their [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - We do not store videos on Google's servers; analysis is real-time

- **Supabase**: User accounts, videos, and analysis results are stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
  - Data is encrypted at rest and in transit
  - Located in [specify region, e.g., US East]
  - Supabase's privacy practices: [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

- **Vercel**: Backend API is hosted on Vercel serverless infrastructure
  - Handles authentication and API routing
  - Does not permanently store user data
  - Vercel's privacy practices: [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
